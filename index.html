About Us

Welcome to Chasing The Winter Adventures, your ultimate destination for adrenaline-packed snowboarding trips! Whether you're a seasoned pro looking to conquer new peaks or a first-time rider seeking the thrill of fresh powder, we’ve got you covered. Our expertly crafted trips blend breathtaking mountain landscapes, top-tier instructors, and the perfect amount of adventure to make every experience unforgettable. Strap in, and let’s hit the slopes—your next snowboarding adventure awaits!

Learn More

The wide variety of benefits with choosing us on your next adventure.

  1. Educational opportunities to learn how to snowboard and ski with our top of line instructors from all around the globe.
  2. Competition training ranging from beginner level intra-school competitions to advanced obstacle course challenges to keep the thrill of the ride going.
    • Intra-school competitions organized by the winter sports association to give students a friendly competition and learn from each other.
    • End-of-season tournaments to show off your progress and win prizes.
    • Obstacle course challenges for those looking for a challenge and enjoy the high speed thrills with our advanced moguls, power riding obstacles, snow park showdown, and much more!
    • Terrain park team jam events consisting of 3-5 riders perform tricks in the terrain park, with the highest team score based on individual and team creativity.
  3. Social meet and greet events to bond with the community and enjoy some relaxing time with our movie nights, potlucks, and more social events.
  4. Hassle free vacation as we also offer transportation to pick you and your family up when staying at the hotels near the ski resorts.
